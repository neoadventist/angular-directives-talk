BetterCare is a digital care plan and care coordination platform for home care agencies. BetterCare helps agencies lower costs, save time, and improve patient care and customer retention.

Automate

Save time and money by automating new patient intake and digitizing patient assessment and care delivery tracking. Long-term care can be viewed and tracked to effectively improve patient care and demonstrate success in key metrics.

Simplify

No more binders, faxing, or burning CDs. Digital Care Plans allow users to view care information easily over the Internet, even using their phone or tablet. Care Plan changes/updates are made in one place and are immediately visible to everyone in the patient's care ecosystem in real time.

Grow

Consumer are increasingly more tech savvy and expect care information to be easily accessible. Improve customer satisfaction and retention by easily sharing care reports. Give them piece of mind with automated notifications letting them know their loved ones are being taken care of.
